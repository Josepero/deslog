H.S.E Risk Management and Consultancy

We provides consultancy services in Occupational Safety & Health, Environment, Security and industrial hygiene. Our team are a staff of highly trained and seasoned Certified Safety Professionals with proven experience in developing safety policy and programs for all types of industries in Nigeria and beyond. We have the machinery and technical know-how to handle accident investigation and reporting, safety auditing, job Hazard analysis, hazards risk assessment, HSE policy and programs for companies of all sizes, safety signage installation, Environmental impact assessment amongst others.

Deslog Energy Services provides Health, Safety and Environment (HSE) and Risk Management consultancy services to the oil & gas and other major hazards industries. We specialise in the management of HSE as well as providing support on industrial hygiene.

Our team are a staff of highly trained and seasoned Certified HSE and risk management professionals with practical experience in consultancy services for all industries in Nigeria and beyond

  • Occupational Health, Safety and Environmental Audit
  • ISO 9001: 2000 Quality Management System
  • ISO 14001 Environmental Management System
  • ISO 18001 Occupational Health & Safety Management System
  • Control of Substances Hazardous to Health Programme (COSHH)
  • Integrated Management Systems
  • Accident Investigation and Reporting
  • Environmental impact assessment (EIA)
  • Workplace Health and Safety Management Systems
  • The setting up of Safety department
  • The setting up of mini Fire Brigade
  • Emergency Planning and Business Continuity
  • Driver Testing and Certification
  • Preparation of Occupational Health and Safety Manuals
  • Preparation of Occupational Health and Safety Pocketbooks
  • Contingency and Emergency plans and management
  • Organising Health, Safety and Environmental Programme and Week
  • Safety Risk Assessments (including Safety Cases, HAZID/HAZOPS etc)
  • OHSAS 18001 Internal Auditing
  • Preparation for and Defence of 2nd Party Audit (Eg. NIPEX Audits, Clients & HSE Audits)
  • Preparation for and Defence of 3rd Party Audit
  • Providing All Documentations And In-House Training Of HSE Officers To Effectively Run The OHS System
  • HSE Manpower Supply
  • Preparation of CASHES Policy & Manual
  • Preparation of QA/QC & QM Policy And Manual
  • Offshore Safety Permit (OSP) Processing
