Overview

Course Objectives:

Thousands of accidents occur every day. The failure of people, equipment, supplies, or surroundings to behave or react as expected causes most of them. Accident investigations and reporting course is designed to determine how and why these failures occur. By using the information gained through an investigation, a similar, or perhaps more disastrous accident may be prevented. We train how to conduct accident investigations and reporting with prevention in mind.

This training focus on causes and possible prevention measures of accident in a work place. We train our students to international standard, our students are competent to work and work safe in any field and anywhere in the world.

Training and Learning Methods:

Training will be delivered in our offices and training centers by our staff of highly trained and seasoned certified safety professionals with proven experience in training and consultancy practices. The course has a theoretical element as well as practical sessions.

Assessment Methods:

Examination will be written under clocked supervised conditions. Daily continues assessment will be taken during the training

Course Duration:

2 Days

Minimum Entry Requirement:

OND, HND, Bsc. and Advanced educational qualifications, HSE level 1,2&3.

Certification:

Internationally recognized Accident Investigation & Reporting will be issued at the end of the training and examination.
