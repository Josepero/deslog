Who we are

International oil and gas, quality Health Safety and Environment Training provider

We are passionate about what we do and our strength and continued growth lies in the quality of our certified professional staff and management's commitment to offer statutory training, test and inspection services that meet our client's requirement of international and local standard. With several years of experience we have helped many companies including multinationals reach global accepted standards by training their staff to be up to date with the latest methods and good practices - and to consistently adopt the "right attitude" - in the fields of quality, health, safety and environmental protection

Training and Certifications

We offer range of oil & gas, HSE and management courses and issue certificates that are accepted all over the world: our trainings are comprehensive, cost-effective and most times tailored to meet organizational specific needs. Whether small or large, local or multinational organization we can offer you specialized courses based on real-life situations.

Inspection, Test and Corrosion Control

Deslog Energy Services Limited (DESL) is a leading company that provides lifting inspections, non destructive testing and corrosion control with the use of wide range of advanced technologies to meet customers’ requirement in accordance to national and international standards. We have built capacity for effective and timely delivery at both offshore and onshore locations. Our engineers are highly trained and seasoned professionals with many years of field experience, they works round the clock to ensure our clients’ satisfactions

QHSE and Technical Consultancy

Our QHSE and technical consultancy services are provided by a team of experts, renowned engineers with hands-on expertise in helping you address issues relating to quality, risk, health and safety, environment, technology and social responsibility needs to meet your business goals. Our project and program management experts can assure a smooth implementation of the recommended changes and lead you through a successful transformation.
