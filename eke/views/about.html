Welcome to Deslog Energy

Deslog Energy Services Limited (DESL)is an international oil and gas training organization.A leading accredited training and certification firm that provides world class HSE, Oil & Gas and Management training services as well as inspection and consultancy services to the oil and gas, marine construction and other related industries.Also part of our portfolio include support services to the oil & gas industry such as environmental support services, equipment & chemical procurement, corrosion control & monitoring, manpower outsourcing and supply.

We have a staff of highly trained and seasoned professionals, certified trainers and experienced engineers working round the clock in achieveing our esteemed clients satisfaction.

Following our quest for quality, Deslog Energy Services has been recognized as a fast growing and most dynamic company in Nigeria with operations across all areas of the African continent and partners across the world. We utilize the most advanced equipment in delivering our services. Our training centers are technologically equipped which has consistently enabled us to be the company of choice for training and certification services.

Deslog Energy Services Limited is registered with Federal Republic of Nigeria under the auspices of Corporate Affairs Commission (CAC) with the registration number RC: 1209184. We are also registered with Department of Petroleum Resources (DPR) with the registration number: DPR/OGISP/16/52288388/N42162 and National Industrial Safety Council of Nigeria (NISCN) with the registration number: 000306, including so many other operational certifications and partnerships obtained locally and internationally.

Deslog Energy continues to break new grounds by investing in cutting-edge technology and equipment, highly skilled personnel, and we are committed to the development of Local Content. We leverage on our leading position in many of our service segments to offer support services to our clients for a cost-effective service delivery.
