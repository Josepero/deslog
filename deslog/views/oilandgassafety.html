Overview

Course Objectives:

Oil and gas environment is considered as the most hazardous environment to carryout operations, so safety of personnel and equipment are highly needed in such environment.This course introduces you to oil and gas safety practices, oil and gas operational safety including safety of all activities involved in the extraction of crude oil and natural gas from reservoirs found beneath the seafloor.

This course was developed as a source of information and practical training tool for rig safety personnel, operator/contractor employees, supervisors, and managers who need to be familiar with rig safety.

Once you complete this training, you will know more about the following components:

  • General safety best practices applicable to all oil and gas facilities
  • Types of personal protective equipment (PPE) needed to keep workers safe
  • How to prevent falls onboard in a oil rig
  • Components of an Emergency Action Plan (EAP)

During training, students are exposed to true knowledge of oil and gas rig activities and their safety. This course is taught using state-of-the-art training materials, allowing for realistic training in a controlled environment.

Training and Learning Methods:

Training will be delivered in our offices and training centers by a team of staff and consultants made up of highly trained and seasoned certified professionals with many years of field experience in oil and gas rig operation. This course comprises of theory and practical section.

Assessment Methods:

Examination will be written under clocked supervised conditions. Daily continues assessment will be taken during the training

Course Duration:

2 Days

Minimum Entry Requirement:

SSCE and above, General HSE and Advanced HSE or equivalent certificates

Certification:

IADC recognized certificate will be issued by Deslog Energy Services Limited in collaboration with Greatwall Drilling Company, Abu Dhabi, Dubai (UAE).

Course Outlines

For Whom

This course is suitable for safety managers,site superintendent or supervisors, oil and gas safety committee member, or someone who is entering into oil and gas career who desire to work in the oil and gas rig environment.This course will help you understand your important responsibilities.
