Overview

Course Objectives:

Fire safety course focuses on the need to respond swiftly and safely to a fire case, the skill is required by personnel working in any industry including homes. It is designed to give a basic level of competency in fire fighting and comprehensive knowledge of how to use basic fire fighting equipment to fight and control fire should in case it occurs.This training is paramount in the oil and gas industry.

Training and Learning Methods:

Training will be delivered in our offices and training facilities by our staff of highly trained and seasoned certified safety professionals with proven experience in training and consultancy practices. This course consist of theory and practical section using fire fighting equipments on live fires

Assessment Methods:

Examination will be written under clocked supervised conditions. Continues assessment will be taken during the training

Course Duration:

1 Days

Minimum Entry Requirement:

SSCE and other above educational qualifications.

Certification:

Internationally recognized Basic Fire Fighting certificate will be issued at the end of training and assessment

Course Outlines

The course syllabus consists of 21 topics (sub-courses):

  • Actions to be taken in the event of a fire
  • Classes of fire
  • General good housekeeping
  • Methods of fire spread
  • Practical use of fire fighting equipment
  • The theory of combustion
  • Types of extinguishers

For Whom

All personnel working in any industry and sector of economy
