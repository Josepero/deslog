Overview

Course Objectives:

The most common cause of injury and death in the oil & gas offshore and onshore site or on any construction site is falling from height. This course is designed to give a high level of competency in scaffold safety.

This course is paramount in the oil and gas and Construction industries where working at height is inevitable. The course focuses on safety and Fall Prevention methods for the scaffolding and access industry. We delivered this course in accordance with awarding bodies guideline and approved codes of practice.

The course is taught using state-of-the-art training modules, allowing for realistic training in a controlled environment.

Training and Learning Methods:

Training will be delivered in our offices and training centers by our staff of highly trained and seasoned certified safety professionals with proven experience in chemical Handling. This course is a mix of theory and practical demonstration.

Assessment Methods:

Examination will be written under clocked supervised conditions. Daily continues assessment will be taken during the training

Course Duration:

2 Days

Minimum Entry Requirement:

SSCE and above educational qualifications, General HSE level 1&2

Certification:

Internationally recognized Scaffold Safety certificate will be issued in collaboration with Deslog Energy Services Limited.

Course Outlines

For Whom

All Personnel who work at height with the use of scaffold Personnel who carry out operation in the oil & gas and construction industries All personnel who wish to start a career in scaffolding
