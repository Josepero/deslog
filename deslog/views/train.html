Training and Certification

Deslog Energy Services Limited (DESL) is an international oil and gas training organization, a leading training and certification providing company in Nigeria, for several years we have been providing accredited and specialized training services to corporate organizations and individuals who carry out operations in the oil and gas industry, marine, power generation, construction, and other related industries in Nigeria and beyond. Deslog Energy Services Limited though operates from her base in Nigeria but has maintained well-built partnerships with high profiled organizations and industries in the UK, Dubai, Qatar, US, Asia and other African countries. Furthermore, we have a staff of highly trained and seasoned certified professionals, high profiled corporate trainers, field experienced engineers and other personnel in various fields who team up to provide the best value to our clients using standard materials and state-of-the-art facilities. In quality service delivery, we are rated higher among our competitors and have ranked number one (No.1) position in Nigeria for almost a decade.

We train candidates to be Professionals in Health Safety & Environment, Oil & Gas technical and engineering skills, Management & Leadership skills and also help professionals to build career in the Oil and Gas Industry and other sectors of economy.

Our Training Delivery Methods

Public Training (Week Days)

  • These courses are taught face- to-face on week days by our certified, highly qualified and experienced trainers.
  • Course dates are scheduled and published online and offline in advance. This type of training is open to everyone who is interested both company delegates and individuals.
  • Comprehensive range of quality course materials is available for learners to take away.
  • Within the classroom environment, students from diverse backgrounds can interact with each other.

Public Training (Weekends)

  • These courses are taught face- to-face on weekends by our certified, highly qualified and experienced trainers.
  • Perfect for people who can’t take time out of their busy week day to attend a course such as workers and students
  • An inexpensive way to study for industry-leading qualifications.
  • The courses are delivered exactly like the public week day learning courses but during weekends only.

Corporate / In-House Training

  • We can visit your organization and deliver face-to-face training at your suite or you can also send delegates to our facility for group training
  • Cost-effective option when you have a group of learners
  • Select your own course dates and tailor the training to fit your company’s needs.
  • Courses are taught by our expert educators in familiar surroundings.
  • This method will reduced travel and accommodation costs for delegates
  • We provide comprehensive, high quality course notes for learners to take away.
  • Discount is given based on the number of perticipants

Customized Training:

  • These courses require the delivery of the same content with other delivery options within a shorter period which means we may spend longer/shorter time in the training and also reduce the duration
  • Course materials provided are available for learners to take away and study in advance.
  • Assessment or examination for these courses is conducted within the period of two months following training to allow participants to prepare adequately for certificate courses.
  • Students are allocated a personal tutor to support their studies and can access online forums to discuss with fellow students.
